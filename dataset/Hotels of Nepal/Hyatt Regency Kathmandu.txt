Hyatt Regency Kathmandu, Kathmandu
The Hyatt Regency hotel in Kathmandu is one popular accommodation in Nepal is stands just close to the Boudhanath Stupa, the UNESCO World Heritage Site. Covering an area of 37 acres this property boasts of excellent service and hospitality inspired by the Newari Nepali culture.

280 spacious rooms are idealized to be perfect for a family stay where amenities such as television, air conditioning, separate showers and tea coffee makers are available. You can choose from the 7 luxury suites and 25 rooms to experience the to-notch facilities offered at Hyatt Regency Kathmandu. There are dining options available for the guests to relish the authentic taste of Nepal. The café, The Terrace, Rox Restaurant, Lobby Lounge and Rox Bar are the best-known eateries of Kathmandu that can add on a zest to your staycation at this best hotel in Nepal, Kathmandu.

Location: Tarragona, Boudha, Kathmandu, Bāgmatī, Nepal
Hotel Ratings: 4 stars
Nearest Airport to the hotel: Tribhuvan International Airport is just 2.6 km away to the Hyatt Regency Kathmandu.
