The Soaltee Kathmandu
With a thoughtful blend of royal Nepali culture and luxury, the Soaltee Kathmandu will make you wake up to the majestic mountains for sensual delight. The resort consists of 285 uniquely designed well-equipped heritage rooms and boasts of its young and dynamic team to deliver excellence to the guests. With a plethora of restaurants and bars best known for the local delicacies and international delights, you can undertake an incredible culinary journey at one of the best places to visit in Nepal. Moreover, the team never fails to host your social and business events in a successful way.

Location: Tahachal Marg, Kathmandu 44600, Nepal
Nearest Airport: Tribhuvan International Airport, Kathmandu
