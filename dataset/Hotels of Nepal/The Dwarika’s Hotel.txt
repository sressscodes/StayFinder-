The Dwarika’s Hotel
Considered one of the best luxury hotels in Nepal, the Dwarika’s hotel in Kathmandu will let you witness its grandeur reflected on its architecture with historic carvings. With over 50 rooms and 48 suites, the hotel can promise you top-notch hospitality for an endearing experience. Marvel at the enchanting landscape and soak yourself in the divine nature. Dwarika’s farm-to-table experience is insightful, and you should explore the exquisite gastronomic scene. If you are into Japanese cuisine, visit the Mako’s Japanese restaurant to savor the Japanese flavors for a lifetime.

Location: Battisputali Rd, Kathmandu 44604, Nepal
Nearest Airport: Tribhuvan International Airport, Kathmandu
